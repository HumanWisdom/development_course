Our story

Dr Manoj Krishna

Founder, HumanWisdom

HumanWisdom was founded by Dr Manoj Krishna. He used to be a spine surgeon, but moved by the suffering of children in war zones he left his medical career and started the HumanWisdom Project in collaboration with other professionals - to help each person access their own wisdom, which comes from a deeper understanding of themselves. He is the author of two books - Understanding Me Understanding You, and Stress Free.

Our Why

Our passion is to help each person live their best life, and make the world a better, more peaceful place. Our How

With more than 60 bite-sized modules we help users feel better now, and then take them deeper to understand the root cause of problems, for long-term benefit. This deeper understanding awakens wisdom and compassion, which can be life-changing. The understanding is simple, but the impact can be profound - for ourselves, and the world.
