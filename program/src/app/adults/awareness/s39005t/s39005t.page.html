Consider an example. Say you are telling me about your holiday - that's layer 1.

In layer 2, I'm reacting to what you are saying by feeling jealous, and blaming you for how I am feeling. Or I am feeling guilty for feeling jealous. I cannot help having this feeling or seem to control it.

I may be getting angry with you and we stop being friends because you have made me feel bad.

That's the extent of my awareness usually. Layers 3 and 4 are hidden from me, so I do not know what is going on there.
