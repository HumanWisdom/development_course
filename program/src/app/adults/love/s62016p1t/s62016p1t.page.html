Words can be deceptive. The word love is not love, just as going to a restaurant and reading the menu is not the same as eating the food. I can say I love you, but if I do not follow that with action, you are not going to feel loved. I may even convince myself I love you, and believe that telling you that is enough. If you ask yourself how you want to be loved by another, what would you say?
Perhaps love is kindness, consideration, putting the needs of others above your own, accepting the other person as they are and so on. It's not just a word, it's what you do for others that counts
