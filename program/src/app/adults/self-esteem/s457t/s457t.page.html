Welcome to this session where we are going to explore what we can do to develop a healthy self-esteem, so we can avoid the many problems having a low self-esteem can cause. If you do have low self-esteem, you may not know what to do to change that.

Perhaps all we need to do is just observe all the unconscious patterns of thinking that contribute to low self-esteem, and explore them one by one, using our intelligence. Change follows naturally as you shine a light on them.
