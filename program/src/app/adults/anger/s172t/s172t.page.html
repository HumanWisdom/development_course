Sometimes people get angry with themselves. The causes of this are complex and include previous trauma and not meeting their own expectations. They may have low self-esteem and feel bad about their behaviour which they can't seem to control. Or, they are generally unhappy and do not understand why. They can express this frustration through self-harm and its a growing problem in young people. We explore the roots of this negative self-talk in the modules on comparison and self-esteem. This understanding can bring freedom.
