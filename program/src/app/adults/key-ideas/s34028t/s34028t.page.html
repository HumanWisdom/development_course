Change comes from exploring and understanding the fact, not from trying to change it. This will feel like a very new idea, so please keep an open mind as we explore it - you can then find out if it is true or not for yourself. When someone is jealous and you tell them not to be jealous, it doesn't work. The same goes if they are stressed, or anxious or angry. In fact it can create more stress because you cannot achieve the goal you have set for yourself of not being jealous and can end up feeling feel guilty. If instead you accept your jealousy completely and explore what lies behind that feeling, without judging yourself as a bad person, you discover that the mind compares all the time. As you begin to observe and understand that fact, and how it causes jealousy, you find one day that the feeling has shifted, and you no longer feel jealous.
