When we are completely enveloped by the fog of emotional pain, it is useful to remind ourselves that all pain eventually passes, even though it may not feel like that right now. I have met people who suffered the most unimaginable pain, in one case, a couple who lost their son to suicide. Even they, eventually, recovered and went on to live a full life. Whatever the circumstances, you will recover and move on. You will find other ways to meet your emotional needs, whether it's a new relationship, or a new job, or a new hobby. A positive attitude will help enormously.
