We are keen to protect the image we have cultivated for ourselves, and that applies also to companies and countries. What others think of us, shapes our lives in a number of ways. In many societies in the world people spend far more on weddings than they can afford, just so others will have a good opinion about them. Families try to outdo each other and it becomes a reflection of self-importance, money and prestige. In other cases companies and politicians hire PR companies to protect their image. These companies may start a campaign to influence what we think of a particular country or company. Why does it matter so much?
