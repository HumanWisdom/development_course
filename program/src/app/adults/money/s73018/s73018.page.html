How do our hidden patterns of thinking impact how we think about money?

7. Pleasure:

Money buys pleasure which can come from buying things or new experiences. Our mind carves pleasure and can never have enough of it, so we think the more money we have the more pleasure we can have, and the happier we will feel. This can be a trap. All pleasure is fleeting, and the pursuit of it can wear the mind down. It can sometimes lead to addiction.
