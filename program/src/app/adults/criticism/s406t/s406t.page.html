We don’t even know where these ideas came from. They just appeared in our thinking one day. If you look carefully though you will find they come from your various influences - your parents, or friends, or social media or the culture you grew up. These ideas seeped into your thinking via the back-door without you realising it, and one day you woke up thinking that they were your ideas, but they weren’t. That's the nature of conditioning. Its not your fault - just the way the mind works. The unconscious process of comparison may also be responsible - if we are not aware, we compare ourselves with others all the time, and in this age of social media, even more so’
