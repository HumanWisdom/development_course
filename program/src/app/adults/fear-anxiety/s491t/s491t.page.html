1 in 5 adults in some countries suffer from anxiety. The main symptoms are a feeling of dread, racing thoughts, poor sleep and appetite, and being irritable. It can also cause physical symptoms like a dry mouth, a tightness in the chest, sweating, fast breathing, tiredness, and dizziness. It can be quite debilitating and can lead to depression. Adults caught in its grasp struggle to find a way out. Trying not to be anxious does not work. Behind all anxiety is the process of fear. A deeper understanding of it may allow us to avoid it and overcome it or manage it more easily
