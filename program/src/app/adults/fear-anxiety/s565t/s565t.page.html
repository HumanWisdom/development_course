Welcome to this session where we are going to explore layer 3 of this pyramid, and the mechanism or process of thinking behind fear, which operates in the background, beyond our awareness.
It is a primal human emotion, which is present in all animals as a protective instinct, and creates the fight or flight response, with secretion of hormones that ready the body to face a threat. It was perhaps created to protect our physical body against physical threats. It has however evolved to also protect us against psychological threats to our sense of self or the ‘I’, and that is where all the confusion begins. A deeper understanding of what is going on allows us to respond with intelligence
