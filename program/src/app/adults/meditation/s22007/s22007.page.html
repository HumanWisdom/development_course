What are the different types of meditation?

01. Vipassana - guided silent retreats. Buddhist in origin.

02. Transcendental meditation - you chant a mantra repeatedly

03. Mindfulness - become aware of your thoughts and actions without judgement

04. Loving-Kindness - think loving thoughts of people, and the world

05. Guided - using a recording by a teacher

06. Self-enquiry - understanding the nature of the Self

07. Spiritual - as part of prayer

08. Body-scan - slowly tightening and relaxing your muscles
