FAQs

1. Is this network marketing?

Yes. Instead of paying the software giants for advertising, we reward our partners instead, who will work with us to introduce this to others.

2. Is my income guaranteed?

No. It depends on sales of the app, and each person introducing other partners to join the program. There are no targets to meet.

3. Do I need to do any selling?

No. All you need to do is introduce the app to your network. It is free to browse. We will give you the artwork to do this. You can also introduce it to organizations: schools/colleges/companies/government departments.

4. Do I need to do any selling?

To become a partner ask them to click on the Partnership Program page on the app, and become a partner using your referral code, or send them this link.

5. Do I need to do any selling?

Other than buying an annual subscription to the app there is no cost. To remain a partner and keep getting your income from other partners, you need to keep your subscription active.

6. Do I need to do any selling?

There is no pressure and no targets to meet. The more active you are, the greater your income of course. You can also work with other partners and support them to reach other organizations.

7. Do I need to do any selling?

No. We will not share your details with others. You will only know the people you introduced, and who introduced you.

8. Do I need to do any selling?

You can see your revenue report in the app.
