Early signs of an argument

01. You start repeating your point of view

02. You notice your tone of voice going up

03. You feel you need to ‘win’ the argument or convince the other person to change their point of view

04. You talk about unrelated issues from the past

05. You start feeling attacked. It becomes an ego issue

06. Your mind goes into reactive mode. All intelligence gets bypassed.

07. You start becoming personal or generalise. ‘You always…’
