In this session we are going to explore the art of speaking and how we can do so with intelligence, integrity, and compassion, so others understand us clearly and what we say is appropriate to the situation. The more we explore the subject the more there is to learn. Words are powerful and can help us communicate clearly, inspire people, be a source of comfort and bring joy, but can also be used to humiliate and hurt others, and can incite people to violence. We need to use them carefully, with wisdom. There is so much to discover. Let's get started
