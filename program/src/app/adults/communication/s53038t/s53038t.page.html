Though we use language to communicate and we are going to talk a lot about listening and speaking, we need to recognise that language has its limitations. If you walk into a room and are at all sensitive, you can pick up the energy in the room straight away - the peace in it if it is a place of prayer, or the disturbance in it if people are stressed, even if no one says a thing. Similarly if you are with someone who is angry, you can pick up that energy even if they don’t say a word. How can you get a feeling of a person without a single word being exchanged? The mechanism behind this remains a mystery. You can nurture this sensitivity through exercises for a quiet mind. It’s helpful in communication. It also means we can’t fake what we are feeling. The other person can usually pick that up.
