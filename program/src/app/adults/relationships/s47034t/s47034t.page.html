How do our relationships with our friends differ from those with our romantic partners and spouses? In some cases we seem to take our friends less for granted, are often kinder to them, hold fewer grudges against them, and more importantly have fewer expectations of them. In some cases we have less conflict with our friends and treat them better than our spouses or partners. Why is that?
