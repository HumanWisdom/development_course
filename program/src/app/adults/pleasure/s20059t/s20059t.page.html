In this example, say, I work in a hospital and do a good job. I get increasingly frustrated and unhappy with my employers because I feel no one acknowledges my worth, or recognises the work I do. I blame my employers for how I am feeling.

Hidden from my awareness my frustration comes from my own need to feel important and if I explore that further, I realise that brings pleasure and makes me feel good.

The need to feel good comes from my own inner emptiness and boredom, which I am unaware of.
