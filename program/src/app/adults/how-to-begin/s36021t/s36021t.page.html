A conversation is a great place to begin our journey of self-awareness and understanding. Just notice what happens and be curious.

When you notice the other person is interrupting you when you are speaking - that is awareness. When you realise that you are doing the same, that's self-awareness - and not easy.

Did you notice also that when a response formed in your mind and you were waiting for the right gap to speak, you stopped listening?

And lastly, did you notice yourself scanning the conversation for something interesting, otherwise you switched off and were only half-listening? You could then continue and ask why we prefer to speak rather than listen. When we are curious and observant there is so much to learn about ourselves.
