Bad things happen to all of us. How can we move on from the past, so it does not impact the present in ways that do not serve us?

The first step is to keep a diary and write down the many different ways past bullying has shaped your thinking, and your actions - and if that serves you today.

Are you more wary of people now, are you quick to react to any perceived criticism, or do you bully others and are not aware you are doing so?

Your self-esteem which was damaged by bullying may not have recovered, or you may be overeating to compensate for the distress you still feel.
