If, despite talking to the person, their behavior does not change, the next option is to talk to someone.

It could be a friend just to share what you are feeling because that will make you feel better.

Or go and speak to someone in authority. A manager in an office, or the Human Resources Department, or another colleague, or a lawyer, or the police. Do it in as calm a voice as you can. Keep a diary of what has been happening and share this with others.
