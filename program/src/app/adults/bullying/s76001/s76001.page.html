Bullying

Welcome. Bullying affects between 20-30% of people and impacts their wellbeing and happiness. We are going to explore the impact of bullying, why people bully others, how it can be prevented, and how we can respond with our innate wisdom. Remember the basic principles of enquiry:
  • Be curious and have a mind open to learning
  • Pause judgement of yourself and others during this enquiry
  • We are just looking and learning about ourselves
  • Deep down our minds function in similar ways
