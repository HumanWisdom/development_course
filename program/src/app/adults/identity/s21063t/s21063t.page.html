If we wanted to explore being simply human what could we do? We can see in this pyramid which represents the layers of our thinking that our lack of inner peace comes from layer 4. The nature of the ‘I’ is restless, empty and not at peace with itself. Our various identities meet our emotional needs which cover over this emptiness. We have explored in the art of enquiry and the inner boredom module how we can meet our inner emptiness, restlessness and boredom without language, by being with it, exploring it, and not running away from it. In doing so it transforms to a sense of peace. If we live with this sense of peace, which comes from the inside, then our attachment to our various identities naturally drops away, without effort. If we want to live in a peaceful world, we need to begin with ourselves
