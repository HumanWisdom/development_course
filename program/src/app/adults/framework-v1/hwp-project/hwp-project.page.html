The Human Wisdom Project

The Human Wisdom Project aims to bring human beings together to explore our shared humanity and live with wisdom. Our mission is to reduce suffering and make the world a better place. This wisdom comes from a deeper understanding of ourselves and how our minds work. As we begin to...
learn about ourselves we see that deep down we are the same human being and our minds function in similar ways. This understanding also awakens compassion, and allows us to live with a sense of peace. Most problems that defy an easy solution begin in our thinking. These include mental health problems, conflicts in our relationships, addiction, violence, war, and climate change. To address them we have to go deeper to explore where they begin, and understand ourselves. Living with wisdom allows us to prevent and more easily overcome these problems, and live in peace, with ourselves, each other and the earth.

The HumanWisdom App

The HumanWisdom app is a life-companion and a step by step guide to help users understand themselves and live with wisdom. This is not based on any ideology, but on looking and learning about ourselves. It has 59 modules that can help you navigate all of life’s challenges with calm. New content is added regularly and there are many features to explore.

Wisdom Programs for Children and Parents

We are developing 3 other wisdom programs - one for children 11 and under, one for teenagers, and one for parents. The adult program is suitable for everyone 16 years and older. Our work with children has shown that they have an innate wisdom that can be easily awakened by asking...
them the right questions. If we can teach them this wisdom, alongside their other subjects, they can meet the challenges of life without getting anxious or stressed, and have healthy relationships. It also helps them develop emotional intelligence and the soft skills they need for work. We are working with schools to develop these programs for children.

The Human Wisdom Foundation

We plan to set up a HumanWisdom Foundation, a charitable organisation funded by HumanWisdom. We will translate the app into other languages and make it accessible to disadvantaged communities around the world. We will set up a teacher training academy to train teachers to teach this wisdom to children. In...
keeping with our ethos to make the world a better place, we will plant a tree for every subscriber. We believe that if we can help more people live with wisdom, we can begin to address some of the most difficult problems humanity faces. The Foundation will work towards this.

Help us Improve

We believe the app belongs to all its users, and everyone is welcome to contribute to help us improve it. It could be an insight into how the mind works, or a better way of presenting this information. We have a shared humanity and are just exploring the mind we all share. There is no authority. Nothing is true unless you see it clearly yourself. This understanding brings illumination, which can lead to transformation. If you have a comment that can help us improve the app please email comment@humanwisdom.me
