Habits and Addictions

In this module we explore what makes the mind prone to develop habits it cannot break, and addictions it cannot escape from, the hidden causes of that in our thinking, how we can meet emotional pain with intelligence, and how we can overcome our habits and addictions. This understanding can also help us avoid them.
