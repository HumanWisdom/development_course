Here is an exercise you can try.

The next time you are critical of yourself, see if you can catch that moment and pause.

Do one of the breathing exercises you learned in the section on nurturing a quiet mind.

Just observe both the feeling of envy for example, and the thought, which is critical of yourself for feeling envious.

If you look carefully you may see the process of comparison and conditioning at work.

Now ask why do you think or feel what you do? This allows you to continue exploring the feeling of envy in yourself.

You are the same human being as everyone else - that may make it easier.
