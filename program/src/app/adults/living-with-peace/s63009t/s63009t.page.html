Consider this example to illustrate how our lack of peace comes from within. This pyramid represents the different layers of our thinking. Layer 1 is what is happening in the world, and say I work in a busy hospital. Layer 2 is my thoughts and feelings. I feel stressed because I feel nobody appreciates me and I think it's a thankless job. I blame the job for how I feel. Layer 3 represents the many unconscious patterns of thinking that operate in the background. Here is my unconscious need to feel important. I am not aware of it. It is this need that is behind my distress, not what is happening at work. Below that in layer 4 is the ‘I’ which feels restless and empty, and in need of pleasure to stop feeling uneasy. Once I recognise the hidden layers of my thinking, I can make intelligent choices, and question my need to feel important.
