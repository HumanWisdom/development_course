There are as many definitions of leadership as there are leaders. You could lead an organisation, or a country, in which case you need a vision for what you want to achieve, and then inspire people anslate that vision into reality. People who are inspired by you will follow you, and that is what makes you a leader. If you want people to follow you you have to build a connection with them and look after them. How are you going to do that? In this module we will explore the skills you need to be a great leader
