Crisis management check-list to consider:

01. Breathe. Zoom out. Stay calm.

02. Canvas different opinions from your team.

03. Study the problem. Do not run away from it.

04. Do a risk analysis of the different options.

05. Do the hard thing that is the right thing to do.

06. Avoid getting angry, blaming others, or covering things up.

07. Become aware of your fear, and put it to one side

08. Become aware of your self-interest, and put it to one side

09. Take responsibility
