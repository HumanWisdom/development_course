How can we inspire others to work hard towards the vision we have set out? If you embody all the qualities of a good leader, and are seen to be hard working, fair, and honest, you will earn the respect of others, and that is the first step. You then have to articulate a vision for where you want to get to in such a way that others want to get to the same place themselves. Everyone is looking for work to give them a sense of purpose and you have to articulate that for them. You have to then support people and help them be better versions of themselves, and dream of being even better. You lead by your actions, not always by your words.
