To live with integrity and honesty is no easy thing, especially when we feel we can get away with it. If you can be honest in the smallest of situations, for example if you see a note or coin on the floor, then you can meet the bigger challenges you will face. We think we live with integrity but it is only when it is tested, in that split second, do we know the truth. Our integrity could be tested when we think we stand to lose from being honest - for example, in admitting a mistake, when a small lie might allow us to get away with it. Or, when we are offered an inducement to look the other way, or do something for someone we know. In that split second when we have to decide, is when our integrity is tested. Or when we could make two equally justifiable decisions, but our self-interest is better served by one of them.
