Stress

70-80% of us report being stressed in most surveys, and long-term stress has been shown to damage our health in many ways. We would not put up with a toothache, so why do we accept stress as a part of life? In this module we explore the root causes of stress, so we can avoid it and more easily overcome it, and discover a way of living without it.
