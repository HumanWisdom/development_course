Both these stories are in the public domain and involve people taking their own lives. The first was a well-respected doctor I knew who had no history of mental health problems, had a stable job and a lovely family. He had a patient complaint which was being investigated by the authorities and he was suspended from work while this was happening. He could not cope and took his own life. The second occurred when I was talking to some students in a high school about stress. Suddenly one girl burst into tears and left the room. The teacher told me that she had broken up with a boy a few months ago, and he had taken his life as a result.
