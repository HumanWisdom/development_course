It's all very well to talk about passion, and it sounds romantic, but suppose I don’t know what I love doing? This can happen too. Perhaps we just need to explore many different things, to discover what it is we love doing. Is your fear holding you back from exploring a path that no one may have taken? I’m reminded of Dr Jane Goodall who in 1960, at the age of 26, went to Tanzania to begin working with chimpanzees at a time when women never did such a thing. She spent the next 60 years doing that and he contribution to conservation is the stuff of legend.
