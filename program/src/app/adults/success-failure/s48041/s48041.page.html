How can we avoid these pitfalls of chasing success?

6. You need to be ruthless to get what you want

Our conditioning may also teach us that we need to be ambitious to get to our goal. This can make us ruthless as we climb the ladder of success. This can affect all our relationships, and turn people against us. We always find ways to justify our behaviour. In many organizations how you treat others in achieving your goal is as important as getting there.
