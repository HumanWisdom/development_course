People forget that when chasing any goal there is a chance you will fail.
If you can’t cope with failure, then don’t set out on the road to chasing success. There is a good chance you will meet failure on that journey. How will you react to it? Jan Koum, the founder of Whatsapp was close to quitting many times because the app wasn’t working and no one was using it, but he kept learning and going back again to refine it. The rest is history.
