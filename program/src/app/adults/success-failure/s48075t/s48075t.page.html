The entire Human Wisdom Program is centred around helping you discover your own wisdom, and this comes from looking at and learning about yourself and how your mind works. This allows you to live with an inner intelligence that can illuminate your life so you can live with a sense of peace, doing what you need to do in the world, but not dependent on it for your happiness or well-being. Your idea of what success broadens to include how successful you are on the inside, as a human being.
