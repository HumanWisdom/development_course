All our past influences are stored in our memory - we call that our conditioning. We are not aware we are conditioned and this shapes our thoughts, opinions, aspirations, and beliefs.

If you have grown up in a home where your parents did not work ( as happens in many parts of the world), you may grow up thinking a life on benefits is fine.

Or if both your parents were lawyers or doctors - that is what you want to be in the future.

If you grew up where fear was used to control your behaviour you will regard that as normal in the workplace when you become a manager.

If you have suffered past trauma in a relationship, you may be scared of getting too close to people, or become possessive if you are in one.

If you grew up with little money in the house, your entire life may be spent in the pursuit of it.
