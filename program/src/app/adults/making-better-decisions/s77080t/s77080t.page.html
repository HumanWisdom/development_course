Sometimes we have an inner voice which is telling us one thing, but because that is contradicting what we have decided to do, we tend to not listen to it.

It is worth giving your inner voice space to be heard and consider what it is saying.

If you are a doctor, your sixth sense can often tell you something is not right with a patient even though all the tests are negative. This is true in any field you have experience.

Or in a romantic relationship, your inner voice can tell you that something is not right with a person, and the relationship is unsuitable for you, even though you find that person attractive.

Nurturing the sensitivity to be able to hear that inner voice, and then having the courage to consider what it is saying is an important skill to learn
