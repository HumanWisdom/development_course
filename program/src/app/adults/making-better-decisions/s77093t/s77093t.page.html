Do you speak to others before you make big decisions ? It is often worth doing so, just to get different points of view you may not have considered before.

Someone with more experience than you will have and have fresh perspectives to offer or ask you questions you may not have considered.

It is especially important to seek out those who may be critical of your decision, and listen to them carefully. Our tendency is to avoid them.

In the end of course, it is your decision to make but consulting widely is a sign of maturity and strength, not weakness
