Humans need to feel safe. It is a core human need and influences our decisions in ways we may not be aware of.

When choosing whom to marry we may opt for the person who has more money over what our heart is telling us, or we may pick a job that pays the most even though it may not be doing something we love.

In any relationship the need for security can make us possessive of our partners or friends, and behave in a way that can harm that relationship.

We feel more secure being with people like us, and that can make us join groups where we feel we belong.

Paradoxically, the need for security can create in us a feeling of feeling insecure.
