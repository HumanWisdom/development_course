FAQs

Why have we created this program?

Our intention is to create a community of users who want to help others and make the world a happier place. Rewarding our users who share the app with their friends allows us to keep the cost of the app down.

Is my income guaranteed?

No. It depends on the sales of the app.

Do I need to do any selling?

No. All you need to do is introduce the app to your network or to organisations. It’s like sharing a movie you like with friends. They sign up for the free trial and if they like it, they can subscribe.

How do I become a partner and introduce others?

First, you subscribe to the app. Then you go to the menu (top right in the app) and click ‘become a partner’. You will get a partnership code and link you can share with others.

How can I share my link with others?

You can email people or post the link on social media. Take a screenshot of the app and share it on your social media with your comments and your link.

How can I track my income?

Click on ‘My partnership’ in the partnership program (found in the menu in the app - top right). Here you can access your revenue report.
