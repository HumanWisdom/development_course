How do you know you may have a phone addiction?

1. You lie about how much time you spend on the phone

2. Your loved ones comment on the amount of time you are on the phone

3. You neglect home work, or house work

4. You are constantly checking people’s profiles and endlessly scrolling social media

5. You become isolated and don’t talk to loved ones

6. You check your phone at night

7. You struggle to switch your phone off
