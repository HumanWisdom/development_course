The first step is to realise the importance of kindness - for yourself, for others, and for the world.

The second step is to ask yourself a question - What gets in the way of being more kind? Observe how your mind is self-absorbed, how it is always looking to get its needs met, and how it acts from self-interest - willing to do things for others if it's going to get something back. Observe this without judgment. This does not mean you are selfish, just human. To break the hidden patterns of thinking, we first have to study them, and that is all we are doing.

The third step is then to look for ways to be more kind in your daily life - whether it's a kind word, a smile, thinking of someone in a more positive way, or doing something for someone.
