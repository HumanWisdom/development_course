We are all conditioned by our past influences but are not aware we have been conditioned. Our conditioning becomes our ‘normal’ and we become attached to it - to our opinions, beliefs, and our learned behaviours.

If we have been bullied ourselves we may regard it as normal and even ‘character building’ to bully others, or condone bullying by others.

If we have had a painful past we may carry our suffering with us everyday, and taking it out on others may feel like a form of release.

Or we may have grown up thinking that teasing others who are different is just harmless banter, not releasing the suffering it causes others.
