When we see others being bullied how can we respond? A lot depends on the context. If we are in a position to intervene and stop it, we should. If we donâ€™t we are complicit in what is happening.

Sometimes we may not be in a position to intervene - or we may be afraid of the consequences to ourselves - could we be excluded from the group? We could ask ourselves if we really want to belong to a group where people are being bullied? Being alone is not as frightening as you may think - and you will make new friends.

Fear is just a state of mind, and we can overcome it by exploring other ways of thinking about the situation.

Is there something else we could do? Perhaps involve others, or speak to someone in authority, or find other ways to support the person being bullied?

Evil flourishes when good people are silent.
