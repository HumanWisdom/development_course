Ways in which you could change:

01. If someone is teasing you about your appearance/beliefs/opinions - accept yourself for who you are and be proud of it. The issue is with them, not with you.

02. Become less sensitive to being teased. Understand when it is just harmless banter.

03. Be clear about your own boundaries and speak up when they are being crossed.

04. Loosen your attachment to your own opinions and beliefs. Accept that there are other ways of being in the world.
