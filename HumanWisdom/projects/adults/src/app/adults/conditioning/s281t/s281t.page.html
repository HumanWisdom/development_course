Here we apply what we learned in the Art of Enquiry, and if you haven't done that module yet, it may be helpful to do so now.

We begin with the 3 steps to enquiry - to first notice and then, accept that our thoughts come from our own thinking, and then question why we think that way.

In this example say I meet you and you have very right wing political views. I start arguing with you and the argument gets quite heated. We both try and convince each other we are right and our relationship gets strained. If I pause and ask why I think the way I do and what's behind the argument, it becomes clear that we have both been conditioned differently and are attached to our respective views. Then I can ask in which other way I've been conditioned, and discover so much more. As a result our argument ends. IT all begins by taking ownership of what I am thinking and feeling and asking whats going on in my thinking to make me think this way. Remember conditioning isnt wrong, but questioning it allows us to respond with intelligence
