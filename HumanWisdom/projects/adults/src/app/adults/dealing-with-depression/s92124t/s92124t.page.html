Continue breathing in slowly filling your lungs with air to a count of 4, and breathe out to a count of 8 with your eyes closed.

Now introduce this new element into the exercise:

As you continue with the exercise, place your index finger in a horizontal manner just under your nose. Notice how the air coming in is slightly cooler than the air coming out. Do this for 1 minute. Now remove your finger and see if you continue to notice that the air coming in is cooler than the air coming out.

Focus on your nostrils and the temperature of the air. Keep observing the thoughts that arise, as if from a distance, without judging them or reacting to them. You are not your thoughts - just your breath.

Do this for 5 minutes
