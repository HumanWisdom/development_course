This is such a useful skill to learn because it can help you deal with negative emotions and thoughts that are unpleasant to bear.

It is based on a simple idea - that behind our negative feelings are negative thoughts. If we can meet a feeling without thinking - just observing it for what it is, without judging it, or even naming it, we can cut off its oxygen supply and we can feel calm straight away.

In the quietness that follows we can see more clearly and make sense of what is happening in our thinking.
