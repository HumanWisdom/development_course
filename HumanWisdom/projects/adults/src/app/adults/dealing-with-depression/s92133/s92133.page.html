Here are some things you could write about every day in your online journal. A daily journaling practice is very helpful indeed.

01. How you felt today

02. What you were grateful for today

03. What goals are you going to set yourself for tomorrow

04. What did you learn about yourself today

05. What is the one kind thing you did today

06. Who did you talk to today

07. What is the one thing you achieved today (however small)
