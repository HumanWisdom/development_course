How common is depression?

01. In 2019 7.8% of adults in the US had at least one episode of major depression (NIMH)

02. The WHO estimates that 5% of people in the world suffer from depression

03. About 60% of people with depression do not seek help

04. It is commoner in women and 18-29 year olds

05. 1 in 3 women, and 1 in 5 men, in the US will suffer from depression by age 65
