After you get better from a bout of depression, you are vulnerable to feeling this way again - especially if life gets challenging. Once you have suffered depression it can come back.

This is why it is important to keep up with everything you have learned about yourself and the illness and stick to the lifestyle changes you have made so far.

Recovering from depression does not always mean you are happy though, and that is another journey you will need to embark on. Review the module on Happiness in the app.

The rest of the app is available to help you on this journey including modules on stress, anxiety, relationships, self-esteem, and meditation.
