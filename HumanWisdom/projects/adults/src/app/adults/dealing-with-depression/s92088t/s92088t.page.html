The person who is depressed may be very self-critical. These negative thoughts keep repeating themselves in a loop in their mind and there seems to be no escape. These thoughts seem real so we believe them to be true - but they may not be - it is useful to remember that.

They may have many regrets about the past and how they behaved, and cannot forgive themselves.

Or they may have feelings of being worthless and less than others, and be critical of themselves for not making more of their life.

They may say things like :

  • I’m not good enough
  • I’ll never be able to feel better again
  • I will never be successful
  • I’m a failure
  • I always disappoint others

    • The first step is to identify these negative thoughts and write them down in your online journal on the app - in the next sessions we will explore how to tackle them
