Money shapes our lives in many ways we do not realise. It's essential for life in many ways, but can also strain relationships, lead to corruption, and make us live with chronic anxiety. Understanding the many hidden patterns of thinking that influence our relationship with money allows us to live with wisdom, so it does not unconsciously direct the course of our life.
