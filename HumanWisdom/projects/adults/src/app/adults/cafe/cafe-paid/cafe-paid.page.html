FAQs

1. How often will I be sent a new connection?

If you get on, and can speak to the same person again, it makes the dialogue easier. To be introduced to a new person drop us an email - team@happierme.app

2. How long will the conversation last for?

We suggest 30 minutes as a starting point. Try to meet once a week.

3. Are there any guidelines for the discussion?

Begin by introducing yourselves. Bring your curiosity and an open mind to the discussion. Discuss a module at a time. The communication module is a good place to begin. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

4. How should I connect with others?

We suggest using Zoom or Google Meet. We will leave it to you to use the platform of your choice.

5. What if there is a problem, or someone is behaving inappropriately?

If you experience any problem, please terminate the call and email us at team@happierme.app

6. How will the group discussions work?

We will introduce you to two or three users via email. Meet once a week, perhaps for an hour at a time. Think of it as a book club, discussing a new topic each week. To be assigned to a new group, email us at team@happierme.app

If you have any questions, email us at team@happierme.app
