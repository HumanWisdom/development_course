Key benefits

Soft skills

50% of hiring managers say graduates lack soft skills such as communication, teamwork, emotional intelligence, critical thinking, and creativity needed for the workplace. These skills are a natural by-product of a deeper selfunderstanding, which is the foundation of the app.

Stress & anxiety relief

Stress affects learning. The app offers immediate help for stress and anxiety , and then explores the root cause, for lasting benefit. This can also boost learning.

Manage your own mental health

A deeper understanding of yourself helps you meet challenges with calm, manage your emotions and your own mental health.

Relationships

Happy relationships enhance learning and lay the foundation for long-term happiness. The app has a detailed section on Relationships , and another section on workplace relationships.

Deal with criticism

The Criticism module helps students accept feedback with a positive attitude, which can make a positive difference to their learning and employability. They also learn how to offer criticism with care.

Enhancing inclusion

The Conditioning module helps students explore and overcome their own prejudices and this can enhance inclusion and reduce conflict between different groups.
