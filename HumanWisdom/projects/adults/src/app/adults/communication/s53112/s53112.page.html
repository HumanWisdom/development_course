#6 Questions to consider before speaking, to help you respond with intelligence

3. How is it going to be received?

This is perhaps the most useful question we could ask before speaking. If we are going to say something critical or sarcastic, we are going to get the same reaction we got the last time. This may make us pause, and find a different way of saying something, or decide to say it at a different time, or hold our tongue, and not say anything at all.
