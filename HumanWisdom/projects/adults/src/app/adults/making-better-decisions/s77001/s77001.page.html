Making better decisions

How can we use our wisdom to ensure that we make better decisions? We make so many decisions that impact our lives - about our careers, our friends, our habits, our choice of spouse or partner, where we live, and how we react to others. In this module we explore all the factors which go into making decisions, including the hidden drivers in our thinking, and how we can make better decisions in our lives.
