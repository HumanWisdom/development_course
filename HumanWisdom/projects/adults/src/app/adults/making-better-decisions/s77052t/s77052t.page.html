Fear is a normal part of being human and protects us from physical harm. As we have explored in the module on fear, our thinking can magnify our fears which operate in the background, shaping our thinking and our decisions.

If I have made a mistake and am afraid of getting criticised, I am much more likely to try and lie my way out of that problem.

Or, if I am afraid of not having enough money, I could work very long hours at the expense of everything else.

My fear of failure may make me afraid of trying anything new, or make the decisions I know are right for my organization

My fear of what others think of me may make me socially anxious and not go out.

My fear of strangers may make me aggressive towards them.
