Our minds are restless and want to constantly stay occupied. We need to be stimulated. This is part of being human.

We are uncomfortable being alone with ourselves and why we may feel lonely,even in a relationship.

This can impact decision making in several ways.

This is why people are on their phones all the time, for example.

This inner restlessness also drives our need for pleasure - whether it is shopping, holidays,drinking, or taking drugs.

The need to have more and more drives most of our decisions.
