We have explored the 3 steps to enquiry before, and are now going to apply it to explore envy.

Noticing I am envious is the first step.

The second step is to accept that feeling as arising in me and taking ownership of it.

I am not going to be critical of myself for feeling it, or the other person for causing it. I'm going to suspend judgment and just observe it.

The next step is the key - and opens the door to wisdom - and that is asking why do I feel this way? What is behind this feeling of envy? Why do I think the other person is responsible for how I feel? How can I respond with intelligence? That leads me deeper to explore layers 3 and 4 as we did in the previous pyramid diagram.
