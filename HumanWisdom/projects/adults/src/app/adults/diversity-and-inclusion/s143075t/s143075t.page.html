If we have accepted the importance to ourselves and our organization of examining our unconscious biases - how do we begin?

We follow the 3 principles of enquiry. Review the modules in the understand yourself section in the pathway program for more details.

We begin with curiosity - just noticing the thoughts that come up when we meet people who are different. We then accept them as coming from our own mind - and not caused by others. And then we ask a question - where do these thoughts, or firmly held beliefs come from? It is the question we ask ourselves that opens the door to wisdom and change
