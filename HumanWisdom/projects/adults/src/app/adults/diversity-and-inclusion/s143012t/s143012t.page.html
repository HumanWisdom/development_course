When employees feel included, they’re more engaged. Highly engaged employees go the extra mile for the organization. This higher engagement has a ripple effect on profitability, team morale, and retention. People working in inclusive workplaces also tend to take less leave for health issues. When companies support D&I initiatives, 83 percent of millennials are actively engaged in their work.

By building an inclusive work environment, you can instill greater trust between employees and leadership一an enormous problem in today’s workforce.
