Our Self-interest operates often beyond our awareness shaping our thoughts and actions. In most situations our mind asks - ‘What's in it for me?’ - but we are not aware of that. It could translate into - How is hiring this person going to help me personally - rather than the organization. I may choose someone who I think will benefit me personally, rather than the organization. It can also impact my relationships with people. It can come in the way of building a more inclusive culture in an organization, and again, just being aware of it in the background, allows us to respond with intelligence.
