The first of these unconscious drivers is our conditioning. All our past influences are stored in our memory and become our conditioning. All our opinions, beliefs and habits are a product of this conditioning. We are not aware we have been conditioned but become attached to it - because it becomes the ‘Me’ - my opinion, my beliefs etc. Because we are not aware we have been conditioned, we never question our opinions - and this is how our biases and prejudices form.

For example, if I have grown up in an environment which was all white and there was regular talk of immigrants in a negative light, that can become my unconscious bias I carry with me for life - unless I am able to understand and question my own thinking.
