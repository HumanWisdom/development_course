We have discovered that our need for external validation often arises because we are not at peace with ourselves and we need something from the outside to make us feel good on the inside. Once we have become aware of this in ourselves and seen the problems it can cause, the next logical step is to explore if we can live with a sense of peace within, and be comfortable being who we are, so our need for external validation reduces and does not drive our behaviour in ways that do not serve us.

The app has plenty of resources to help you do so. Find breathing exercises and a meditation practice in the Develop a Calm mind section, do the Awareness exercises one by one to learn how to understand yourself, and explore the Living with Peace module.
