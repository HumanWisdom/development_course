Why do I feel the need to feel listened to and be important?

Behind our need to feel listened to and important is an emotional need to feel good inside. We have many emotional needs we are not aware of - to express ourselves, be understood, feel loved, feel secure, and so on. We expect others to meet these needs.

Understanding our emotional needs helps us to be happier, have relationships without conflict, and understand others better.
