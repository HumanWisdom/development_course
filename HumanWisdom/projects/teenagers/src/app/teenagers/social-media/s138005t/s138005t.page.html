I was speaking to some students and asked them how social media affected their lives. They said it made them compare themselves with others, feel envious, feel inferior, created unrealistic expectations, made them dislike their bodies, feel stressed, pressured them to conform, and affected their relationships. We can think other people have perfect lives and that can make us have unrealistic expectations of our own lives, which can lead to disappointment.

I said - 'How can an inanimate object, like a phone, make you think and feel and behave in a particular way without your awareness or consent?'. Other people can't make us do anything without a discussion or an argument, so how come the phone does that so effortlessly?

The students had no answer - this module will explore this in more detail
