How do you know you may have a phone addiction?

01. You lie about how much time you spend on the phone

02. Your loved ones comment on the amount of time you are on the phone

03. You neglect home work, or house work

04. You are constantly checking people’s profiles and endlessly scrolling social media

05. You become isolated and don’t talk to loved ones

06. You check your phone at night

07. You struggle to switch your phone off
