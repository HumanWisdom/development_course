Some statistics on social media use in teenagers:

01. 91% of teens use social media

02. Youtube, TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat are the most popular platforms

03. Non-school screen time averages 7.7 hrs/day

04. 24% of teens are addicted to social media

05. Teens check their phones more than 150 times/day

06. 35% of teens feel lonely, despite time on social media
