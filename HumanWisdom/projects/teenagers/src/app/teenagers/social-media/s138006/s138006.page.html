Some statistics on social media use in teenagers:

1. 91% of teens use social media

2. Youtube, TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat are the most popular platforms

3. Non-school screen time averages 7.7 hrs/day

4. 24% of teens are addicted to social media

5. Teens check their phones more than 150 times/day

6. 35% of teens feel lonely, despite time on social media
