Insight is not something the mind can acquire through effort, like running a marathon for example.

Instead all we have to do is set out on this journey of learning about ourselves with curiosity and passion and these insights will surprise you on a regular basis.

It’s like taking a road trip, and as you turn a corner you may be presented with the most wonderful sight that takes your breath away, it’s so beautiful.

Insight is similar, but it also brings transformation. You are changed by it, without realising how that happened. Another example for me was when I realised that I was a poor listener. That awareness itself made me a better listener.
