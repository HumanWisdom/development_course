In the second stage, you have that insight into what is happening in your thinking, in the moment you are in. For example, as I am talking to someone about politics and an argument begins, I see in real time what is happening deep down in my thinking, and in the other person. That in-the-moment understanding shifts something, and I stop arguing, because I see what is going on.

Here, understanding comes, like a flash, while I am talking to the other person, and I see the futility of continuing the argument_
