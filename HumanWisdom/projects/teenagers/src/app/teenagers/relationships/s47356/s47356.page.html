Why do people bully others?

01. They often have no insight into their own behaviour and think their actions are justified.

02. They may have been bullied before and regard that behaviour as normal.

03. They may be emotionally traumatised, and bullying others brings pleasure and a form of ‘release’.

04. They usually have no insight into the emotional pain they are causing.

05. The unconscious need for power, which brings pleasure, usually operates in the background, shaping their behaviour.
