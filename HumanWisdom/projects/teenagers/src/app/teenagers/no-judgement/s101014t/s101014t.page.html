Here is an exercise you can try.

Say someone posts a picture of themselves on Social Media on holiday - showing them having a good time with some amazing pictures of the hotel and the beach and so on.

You immediately judge them for posting those pictures- you may think they are showing off, or deliberately trying to make you feel bad, or they are just posting fake pictures and they are not real.

Do you notice this feeling of automatic judgement in yourself?

Its not right or wrong - can you just become aware of the many times a day you judge others?
