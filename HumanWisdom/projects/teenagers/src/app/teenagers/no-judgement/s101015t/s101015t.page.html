Here is an exercise you can try.

The next time you are critical of yourself, see if you can catch that moment and pause. Say you look at social media and see a friend's post has many more likes than anything you have posted.

Do one of the breathing exercises you learned in the section on nurturing a quiet mind.

Just observe both the feeling of envy, and the thought, which is critical of yourself for not having enough followers.

If you look carefully you may see the process of comparison at work in the background, which is responsible for your feeling of envy. Social media is just a trigger for that feeling.

Now continue exploring the feeling of envy in yourself.

You are the same human being as everyone else - that may make it easier.
