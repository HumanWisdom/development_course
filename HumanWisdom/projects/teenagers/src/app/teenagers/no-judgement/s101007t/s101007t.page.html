Perhaps we can begin by noticing that we judge ourselves as good or bad, right or wrong all the time. It's a pattern that repeats itself. Where does it come from? We could then pause the judgement process and see ourselves as we are.

It is in accepting ourselves as we are that there is peace and the freedom to then look at and understand what is going on.

Every emotion we feel is the same as in all human beings, and that may also help us look at ourselves without judgement.
