This need for external approval in us is so powerful that sometimes our idea of success becomes what others think of us. This often happens without our awareness. We judge others based on how famous they are – even if they are famous for just being famous – the modern social media influencer. On social media the currency of success has become how many followers people have. We can judge the success of our lives based on how much recognition we have had – how many papers I have published if I am a researcher, how much status I have achieved in society, how many awards I have had If I am in public service, and so on. Meanwhile, the mind that seeks external approval to feel good about itself – remains thirsty. No amount of external validation quenches the thirst of the heart for feeling at peace with itself.
