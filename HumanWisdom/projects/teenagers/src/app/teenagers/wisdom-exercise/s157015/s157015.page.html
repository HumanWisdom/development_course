Exercise #14

Being Grateful

Welcome to this session where we are going to explore gratitude. It is a simple thing we can all do to lift up our spirits and appreciate the life we have. It can contribute to our overall sense of happiness and help us get through difficult times. Keep in mind the basic principles of enquiry:

  • We are just looking and learning about ourselves
  • We need to have a mind open to learning
  • For learning to take place we need to pause judgement
  • What we observe in ourselves is not right or wrong, good or bad - it just needs to be understood
  • Deep down, we are the same human being

The mind takes things for granted.
