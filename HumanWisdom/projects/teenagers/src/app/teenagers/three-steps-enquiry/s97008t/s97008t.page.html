The third and perhaps most important step is to question what is going on in our mind to make us think and feel this way?

Continuing with the previous example, once I had accepted ownership of my anger I asked myself what lay behind it.

I discovered that it was as a result of an expectation I was not aware of, that had come from my past influences. I had grown up with the expectation that people in close relationships had a duty to listen to each other. This discovery led to explore other expectations I carried from my conditioning and I learned so much.
