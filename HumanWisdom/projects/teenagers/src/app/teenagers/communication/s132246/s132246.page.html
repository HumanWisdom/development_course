Early signs of an argument

1. You start repeating your point of view

2. You notice your tone of voice going up

3. You feel you need to ‘win’ the argument or convince the other person to change their point of view

4. You talk about unrelated issues from the past

5. You start feeling attacked. It becomes an ego issue

6. Your mind goes into reactive mode. All intelligence gets bypassed.

7. You start becoming personal or generalise. ‘You always…’
