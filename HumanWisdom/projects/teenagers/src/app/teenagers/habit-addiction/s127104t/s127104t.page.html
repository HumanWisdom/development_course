Acceptance is perhaps the most powerful tool you have at your disposal to put out the fire of emotional pain in your mind. So much of our pain is just the difference between how things are and how we want them to be. If you can accept yourself as you are, others as they are, and the world as it is, your pain can dissolve. It is not always possible, or easy.
