What is Wisdom?

Being a teenager can be challenging at times. There are so many pressures to deal with - stress, anxiety, social media, school, exams, friendships, not being sure what the future holds, and sometimes dealing with parents who do not always understand you. You can meet all these challenges much more easily if you have a deeper understanding of yourself - which leads to wisdom.
