Why do I feel envious?

We think others are responsible for our envy, but it could be because our mind compares all the time and we are not aware of it. Envy causes heartache, makes us feel less than others, and pushes us to be like others.

Understanding this process of comparison in our own thinking helps us be free and walk our own road in life.
