Understanding these 4 layers of thinking has many benefits. Normally I would blame you for making me feel jealous. This is because normally I am only aware of layers 1 and 2. Now I can see though that the automatic process of comparison which comes from layer 3 is the real cause of my envy. I stop blaming you for a start, and then ask myself what I can learn from this feeling that has arisen in me... and that leads me to begin a journey of a deeper understanding of myself, which can lead to transformation.
