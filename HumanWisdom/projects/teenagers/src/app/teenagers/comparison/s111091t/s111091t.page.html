Lets explore envy through this pyramid.. At the very top is the thing that is making us envious or jealous, it could be a friend who has been on a great holiday.

The second layer is to notice what we are thinking or feeling and we may notice that we blame the other person for our envy, or may feel guilty.

Then our awareness goes deeper and in the third layer, we notice the process of comparison at work in the background. This is not always easy to spot

The last layer of awareness is one we are going to explore in a separate module, which is to ask - who feels the pain of envy?

Though, for the sake of describing envy I've divided it into 4 sections, you can be aware of all four levels at once. The more you notice, the more you see, and the easier it gets.
