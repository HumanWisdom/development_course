Indulging in pleasure dulls the mind and we feel weary - for example if you watch video games or movies all day, and these activities soon cease to give us pleasure.

The same thing happens if you take drugs or drink too much or go shopping all day.

Notice this in yourself and find out if it is true.

A dull mind loses its sensitivity to the beauty of the world, and its ability to make rational decisions that are in its best interest.

A deeper understanding of the way the mind works leads to a natural sense of peace, and pleasure finds its rightful place in our lives
