Being open with parents or friends is important so they can help us get better.

When we are depressed we tend to withdraw from others - it is worth having an open conversation with other adults we have a relationship with - acknowledging that you are less emotionally available for them now, and that you are going to do your best to be there for everyone, but to ask for their understanding and support as you focus on getting better.

The same conversations could be had at school. Being open with people often brings out the best in them and you will be surprised by the amount of support you get
