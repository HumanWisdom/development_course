How common is bullying?

01. 18% of NHS staff reported being bullied (UK)

02. 20% of teenagers aged 13-18 (CDC)

03. 1 in 6 students were cyberbullied (CDC)

04. 20% of US workers experience bullying (ishn.com)

05. 1 in 3 young people in 30 countries (UNICEF)

The actual rate of bullying is probably higher, as it is under-reported
