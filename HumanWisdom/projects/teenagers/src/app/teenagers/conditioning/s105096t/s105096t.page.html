Life can be tough for many of us, and this may result in the burden of emotional pain.
We carry this pain around with us and this affects our personality, our behavior, and our future.
Or if we have been the victims of bullying, we may be afraid to go out. Is it possible to be free of this emotional pain we carry with us, so that we can live with joy and a sense of peace right now? We seem to have such little control over how we feel. It is okay to feel whatever you are feeling. You can let the pain of your past go, and be free to live fully in the present.
When a memory arises, it may flood your mind with feelings. Just watch it, and allow it to fully express itself. Be still with it as we explored in the art of enquiry.
