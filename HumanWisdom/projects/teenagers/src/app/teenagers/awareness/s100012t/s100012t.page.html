Another example of these different layers at work is the difference between our real motive, and what we tell ourselves and others. Say I'm a politician. I convince myself and others that I am in politics to make a difference and serve people.

That's all I see. Hidden from awareness though in layer 3 are my many emotional needs - for power, to be important, feel secure, and for pleasure.

Deeper still, in layer 4, is my self-interest that operates quietly in the background. None of these are wrong by the way.
