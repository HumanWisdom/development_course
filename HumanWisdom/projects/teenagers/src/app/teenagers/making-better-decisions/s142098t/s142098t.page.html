We often make decisions on incomplete information and that makes for bad decisions. It is worth pausing to ask if you have all the facts to hand before deciding.

For example, when choosing which subject to study in senior school, you may assume that its going to be harder work than it is, or assume you are not capable - and both of those assumptions may not be correct.

Or you may assume that taking drugs is harmless and everyone does that - and that may damage your health.

We also make assumptions about people - for example we may assume someone does not like us, and be mean to them - but that may not be the case.
