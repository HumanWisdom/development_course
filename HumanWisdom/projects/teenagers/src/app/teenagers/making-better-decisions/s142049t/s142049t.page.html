All our past influences are stored in our memory - we call that our conditioning. We are not aware we are conditioned and this shapes our thoughts, opinions, aspirations, and beliefs.

If you grow up in a home where your parents smoke, you are more likely to regard that as an okay thing to do.

Or if both your parents are lawyers or doctors - that is what you want to be in the future.

If you grow up an atmosphere where fear is used to control your behaviour you may regard that as normal and use it with others.

If you have suffered past trauma in a relationship, you may be scared of getting too close to people, or become possessive if you are in one.
