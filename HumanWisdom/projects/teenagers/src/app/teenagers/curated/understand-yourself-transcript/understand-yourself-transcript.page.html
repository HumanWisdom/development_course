No matter how successful we are in the world happiness often eludes us. We struggle to feel at peace with ourselves. Why is that? Here you can find out what you can do to be happier. Explore what happiness means - is it pleasure, or the absence of unhappiness, or is it contentment? What gets in the way of being happy now? We assume happiness comes from the outside and so pursue it through all our activities in the world. We think we will be happy in the future when we get what we want. As soon as we get it however the pleasure begins to fade and we are left feeling empty again, and need to go after something new. Understanding the many hidden patterns of thinking that get in the way of happiness, allows us to be happy now. Come back and review the material here often - you will learn something new each time.
