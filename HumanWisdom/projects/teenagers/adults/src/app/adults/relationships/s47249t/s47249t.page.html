In all societies in the world when people marry there are celebrations and ceremonies to mark the occasion, and this has been the same for thousands of years. People value commitment because it makes us feel secure, and in that security we can grow as human beings and our relationship can flourish. In modern times people often live together and even then they usually commit to being with each other exclusively. Some people are scared of commitment, and others crave it. Commitment is also valuable in friendships, though there it takes on a different meaning
