We are defined by our identity and it brings us comfort, but can also cause conflict, division and war. We explore how our identities come into being, the benefits they bring us, the conflict they can cause, and how we can respond with intelligence and wisdom. In doing so we may discover a sense of peace, and become a beacon of peace in the world.
