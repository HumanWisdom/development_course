Tips to overcome emotional distress

Sometimes, we feel blue, or upset without a reason - or rather we canâ€™t think of a single cause for how we feel.

Maybe we feel a jumble of different things all at once - perhaps we feel lonely, stressed and anxious at the same time? Or feel an ache with ourselves that does not have a name. Or we may feel restless and not know what to do with ourselves. All these feelings are part of being human, so you are not alone.

Whatever the emotion you may be feeling, dealing with it involves the same principles.

We need to find ways to feel better now - and the app offers many options - pick one depending on what works for you - it could be breathing exercise, journaling, an audio meditation or doing some tapping.

Once you start to feel better it is perhaps worth asking what you can do for the long term to keep the blues away.

Beginning a meditation practice is a good way of bringing some order to your emotions, and calming your mind, so it is ready to deal with the challenges of the day.

The meditation module takes you through the basics of meditation, explores what the pitfalls may be and gets you started on your own practice. Meditation at its core is just sitting quietly, noticing your body, your thoughts and feelings and also noticing the observer who is recording everything. There is no pressure, no goal to achieve and no right and wrong way to meditate. Lets get started.
